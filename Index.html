About Sudeep Regmi

With over a decade of experience in mindfulness practices, Sudeep Regmi is dedicated to helping individuals find peace and balance in their daily lives through meditation, yoga, and philosophical discussions.

His approach combines traditional wisdom with modern techniques, making mindfulness accessible to everyone regardless of their background or experience level.
